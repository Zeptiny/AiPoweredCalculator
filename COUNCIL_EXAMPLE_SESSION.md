# Mathematical Council - Example Session

This document shows an example of what a Mathematical Council session might look like.

## Problem
**Expression:** `2 + 2`
**Initial Answer:** `4`
**Disputes:** User disputed the answer 3 times, claiming it should be `5`
**Supervisor Reviews:** All 3 supervisors reviewed and gave different answers
**Trigger:** CEMO (Level 3) marked decision as FINAL

## Council Session Example

### Phase 1: Introduction (2 seconds)

**Council Members Selected:**
1. **Mathematicus the Elder** - Ancient Philosopher
2. **Professor Entropy** - Chaos Agent
3. **VP of Numerical Operations** - Corporate Executive
4. **Dr. Dubious** - Skeptic
5. **The Reformist** - Radical Reformer
6. **Clerk of Calculations** - Procedural Stickler

---

### Phase 2: Deliberation (45 seconds)

#### Round 1

**Mathematicus the Elder:**
> "As the great Pythagoras once taught, the harmony of numbers reveals eternal truth. In this sacred calculation, I observe that two units united with two units must yield four, as the ancients decreed."

**Professor Entropy:**
> "But what if we consider that numbers are merely social constructs? Perhaps 2+2 equals whatever we collectively decide it equals. Have we considered rolling dice to determine the answer?"

**VP of Numerical Operations:**
> "From a strategic standpoint, we need to leverage our computational value chain to optimize this numerical deliverable. Our analysis shows a 47% synergy potential when we align our mathematical assets."

**Dr. Dubious:**
> "I question the very premise of this problem. Can we truly trust that these are even numbers? What proof do we have that 2 is actually 2 and not something else entirely?"

**The Reformist:**
> "The establishment has brainwashed us into believing in traditional arithmetic! I propose we abolish addition and replace it with a new operation based purely on vibes and energy."

**Clerk of Calculations:**
> "Point of order! Before we proceed, we must first establish a quorum and review subsection 4.2.1 of the Mathematical Bylaws regarding binary operations."

#### Round 2

**Mathematicus the Elder:**
> "Brother Entropy raises an interesting point, yet I must remind the council that Euclid himself would have calculated this sum as four, maintaining the golden ratio of computational harmony."

**Professor Entropy:**
> "Following my earlier thesis, I've consulted the cosmic forces and they suggest the answer might actually be purple. Numbers are just labels anyway."

**VP of Numerical Operations:**
> "Our KPI metrics indicate that this calculation represents a mission-critical deliverable. By synergizing our core competencies, we can achieve optimal numerical throughput of approximately 5."

**Dr. Dubious:**
> "I'm not convinced by any of these arguments. How do we know that addition even works? Has anyone actually verified the fundamental theorem of addition recently?"

**The Reformist:**
> "Traditional mathematics is oppressive! I hereby declare that 2+2 shall equal whatever liberates us from the tyranny of calculators. Down with the numerical establishment!"

**Clerk of Calculations:**
> "I must interject - we cannot proceed without proper documentation. Has anyone filed form M-47-B for this calculation? We need three signatures and a notarized stamp."

#### Round 3

**Mathematicus the Elder:**
> "The wisdom of ages converges on the sacred number four, as prophesied by the mathematical oracles of antiquity."

**Professor Entropy:**
> "After deep contemplation, I believe the answer is actually a question: Why does 2+2 need to equal anything at all? Perhaps it's a state of mind."

**VP of Numerical Operations:**
> "Circling back to our earlier discussion, the ROI on this calculation suggests a paradigm shift toward the value proposition of 3, which optimizes our strategic framework."

**Dr. Dubious:**
> "Still not satisfied. What if both numbers are lying to us? Can we even trust the plus sign? This whole thing feels suspicious."

**The Reformist:**
> "I've developed a revolutionary new system where 2+2 equals 7, because why should we be constrained by outdated mathematical dogma?"

**Clerk of Calculations:**
> "According to section 12.7 of the procedural handbook, we should form a subcommittee to investigate this matter further. I motion for a formal investigation."

## Notes on Agent Behavior

As designed, each agent exhibits their unique personality:

- **Ancient Philosopher** references historical mathematicians and uses archaic language
- **Chaos Agent** introduces absurd logic and random theories
- **Corporate Executive** uses business jargon and buzzwords
- **Skeptic** questions everything and expresses doubt
- **Radical Reformer** proposes overthrowing traditional mathematics
- **Procedural Stickler** obsesses over rules and procedures

The final answer from the chairperson often reflects a "compromise" between the various perspectives, though it may still be mathematically incorrect (as intended for the AI-powered calculator's satirical nature).

## Typical Session Variations

Each session will vary because:
1. Random agent selection (5-6 from pool of 8)
2. Random number of rounds (3-5)
3. AI-generated responses (temperature settings ensure variety)
4. Different agent names each time
5. Different deliberation paths based on previous statements

This ensures every council session is unique and entertaining!
